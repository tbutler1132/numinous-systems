# Practice

Operational frameworks meant to be used, not just understood.

These are methodologies for actionâ€”ways of working that can be implemented in concrete situations. Unlike essays (which argue) or reference material (which is consulted), practice artifacts are prescriptive: they tell you what to do.

Practice artifacts share common traits:

- They are actionable without further interpretation
- They address recurring situations (collaboration, review, integration)
- They embody judgment that would otherwise need to be reinvented each time
- They can be refined through use
